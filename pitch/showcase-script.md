# Impact Intelligence — 40-Second Showcase Script

## Voiceover Script (~42 seconds)

> **[0–4s]** Every day, global events ripple into your life — but the noise drowns out what actually matters to *you*.
>
> **[4–10s]** Impact Intelligence changes that. We pull live signals from news feeds, energy markets, and humanitarian reports — and turn them into something you can act on.
>
> **[10–18s]** Our AI engine frames every signal through *your* lens — whether you're a commuter, a student, a farmer, or a small business owner. Each card tells you: what happened, why it affects you, and what to do next.
>
> **[18–26s]** Every insight carries a confidence badge — separating verified facts from inference — with full source citations, so you never have to wonder where the information came from.
>
> **[26–34s]** Filter by persona, category, or region. Get city-level context for Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, and beyond. This isn't generic news — it's intelligence built for *your* reality.
>
> **[34–42s]** Impact Intelligence. Live signals. Local context. Actions you can trust.

## Tone & Pacing Notes

- **Mood:** Calm authority, not hype. Trustworthy, not alarmist.
- **Pacing:** Unhurried first 4 seconds (let the problem breathe), then crisp momentum through the product walkthrough, slow and deliberate on the closing tagline.
- **Voice:** Neutral, warm, confident. Think "calm briefing" not "commercial."
- **Music:** Minimal electronic / ambient. Avoid stock-corporate. Think Tycho or Rival Consoles.
